Purpose:
This policy defines the hiring recommendation criteria used during candidate evaluation.

Hiring Score Thresholds:

Score >= 80
Recommendation: SHORTLIST
Action: Candidate proceeds to Technical and HR Interviews.

Score 60-79
Recommendation: HOLD
Action: Candidate remains under consideration for future openings or additional screening.

Score < 60
Recommendation: REJECT
Action: Candidate does not meet the minimum requirements for the role.

Evaluation Parameters:

1. Technical Skills Match
2. Relevant Work Experience
3. Educational Qualification
4. Project Relevance
5. Certifications
6. Communication and Professional Skills (if available)

Important Notes:

* Recommendations must be based solely on candidate qualifications.
* Personal information must not influence hiring decisions.
* All evaluations should be explainable and auditable.
