Recruitment Rules:

General Eligibility Criteria:

1. Candidate must possess a recognized degree:

   * B.E.
   * B.Tech
   * MCA
   * M.Tech
   * Equivalent Qualification

2. Candidate must satisfy the mandatory skills specified in the Job Description.

3. Candidate experience should align with role requirements.

4. Project experience relevant to the job role should receive additional consideration.

5. Certifications related to the job role may improve evaluation scores.

Screening Rules:

* Missing critical skills may result in rejection.
* Candidates with transferable skills may be considered for alternate roles.
* Internship experience can be considered for entry-level positions.
* Open-source contributions and personal projects may be considered as experience.

Data Validation Rules:

* Empty resumes are invalid.
* Empty job descriptions are invalid.
* Duplicate candidate submissions should be flagged.
